About Me

I am a 28 year old man originally from the Portland, OR area (a rarity these days!). I'm a pretty quiet, laid back person and I tend to open up and talk more to people I feel comfortable with and trust. Growing up I always excelled in liberal arts areas of study such as History, English, Psychology, and Sociology. My educational background is a high school diploma and about two years of a college education. I did well in college but I never really knew what I wanted to do and it didn't seem worthwhile to continue without knowing what my goals were. My work experience includes food service, retail, data entry, manufacturing, and warehouses. I have spent the bulk of my working years in warehouses where I operated forklifts and other heavy equipment. While I excelled at my positions in warehouses, it was never really something I was that passionate about, and I knew that it was not something I wanted to do long term. One thing I noticed about all the jobs I had worked was that I did best when I could work independently, with little supervision. I am an internally motivated person and I can achieve results based on my own drive to succeed and not necessarily external pressure. I did take a web design course in high school and I did quite well in it. This was back in 2010, and the content was only basic HTML, but I remember enjoying the class a lot. This small introduction to coding, combined with an aptitude at problem solving, attention to detail, and a strong memory (and also a desire to earn more money, to be perfectly honest), led me to pursue a career in programming. A good friend of mine went to Epicodus and had nothing but good things to say about it. After teaching myself some basic coding, I decided that it was something I wanted to fully pursue. Here I am!
